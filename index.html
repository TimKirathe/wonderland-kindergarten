ABOUT US

We have began with them from their mother’s lap – we have had the privilege of teaching them their first English words. We toilet trained them, fed them and held their hand as we taught them how to hold a crayon, a paint brush, a pencil…or a book.

We taught them their first nursery rhyme, we were there when they learnt how to climb the slide steps and proudly watched them slide down. Together, we did the first “push-me” at the swings and saw them gain confidence at the see-saw and rocking boats. Lovingly and carefully, we taught them A-Z and 1-100.

We are proud to say that many are now blossoming young doctors, teachers, dentists, nurses, news casters and many more. Others are students of great promise in Universities, Colleges, Secondary and Primary schools.

Yes! By the grace of God we have molded them and continue to do so at Wonderland Kindergarten, where we learn, we play, we care.

We are now approaching our forth decade of service to children and have established a reputation for excellence and thoroughness, in providing quality pre-school education for young children. We continue to do this through an atmosphere of Christian love and culture, based on Biblical values. We give individual attention to every child, making sure they feel loved and safe in an atmosphere of fun, self-fulfillment and adventure.

Today, our teachers are trained in the new curriculum and continue to eagerly prepare our young learners to be effective and productive members of our society through competency based learning.
